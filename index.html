The Scottish Fold is a breed of domestic cat known for its unique physical trait: folded ears. These cats have a genetic mutation that causes their ear cartilage to fold forward, giving them an adorable and distinctive appearance. Scottish Folds are typically medium-sized cats with a rounded head, large expressive eyes, and a sweet, affectionate personality.
